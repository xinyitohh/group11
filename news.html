Latest News

APU Badminton Club Wins Regional Tournament

The APU Badminton Club emerged victorious in the recent regional badminton tournament held in Bukit Jalil. The team displayed exceptional skills and sportsmanship throughout the competition, securing the top position and bringing home the championship trophy.




National Badminton Player Joins University Badminton Club

We are thrilled to announce that Ali, a prominent national badminton player, has joined the APU Badminton Club. Their expertise and experience will undoubtedly contribute to the club's success and inspire our members to strive for excellence.




APU Badminton Club Hosts Inter-University Tournament

The University Badminton Club is proud to host an inter-university badminton tournament next month. Teams from various universities across the country will compete in both singles and doubles categories. This event aims to promote friendly competition and foster camaraderie among university badminton enthusiasts.
