Our

Stories

The APU Badminton Club has a rich and storied history dating back to 2024. Initially formed by a small group of badminton enthusiasts, the club quickly gained popularity among students who shared a passion for the sport. What began as casual matches on campus soon evolved into organized training sessions, friendly competitions, and participation in intercollegiate tournaments.

Over the years, the club has witnessed remarkable growth, both in membership and in its impact on campus life. Through the dedication of its members and the support of the university community, the University Badminton Club has become a cornerstone of sports culture, fostering a sense of camaraderie and sportsmanship among students.

Today, the club stands as a testament to the enduring legacy of its founders and the countless individuals who have contributed to its success. From its humble beginnings to its present-day prominence, the University Badminton Club continues to uphold its tradition of excellence, welcoming new generations of players and inspiring a lifelong love for the sport.

Our Mission

WHAT WE DO

Promote Healthy Lifestyle

Our mission is to promote a healthy and active lifestyle among students through the sport of badminton

Create Inclusive Environment

We strive to create an inclusive environment where members can improve their skills, engage in friendly competition, and develop lifelong bonds with fellow players

Cultivate Badminton Community

To cultivate a community where the joy of badminton transcends mere sport, fostering resilience, leadership, and a sense of belonging among students as they embark on a journey of holistic development.

Vision


Our vision is to become the premier badminton club in the region, known for our excellence on the court and our contributions to the community off the court.





We aim to inspire a new generation of badminton players, fostering a passion for the sport that extends beyond the university.
