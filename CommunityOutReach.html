School Programs

School Visits

Our club regularly visits local schools to introduce students to the sport of badminton. Through fun and interactive sessions, we aim to inspire the next generation of players.

Training Sessions

We conduct training sessions in schools to teach students the fundamentals of badminton and help them develop their skills under the guidance of experienced coaches.

Community Programs

Free Training Sessions

Join us for free training sessions designed for community members of all ages and skill levels. Learn from experienced coaches and improve your badminton skills in a supportive environment.

Community Tournaments

Participate in our exciting community tournaments and showcase your badminton talents. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, there's a tournament for everyone to enjoy!

Our Leading Partners

  • Aestro Malaysia Holdings

    Aestro Malaysia Holdings is a leading company dedicated to promoting sports and healthy living in Malaysia. As our valued partner, they provide essential support and resources to help us achieve our mission of making badminton accessible to all. Through collaborative efforts, we aim to inspire individuals of all ages to lead active and fulfilling lifestyles.

  • IOI Groups

    IOI Group is a renowned conglomerate known for its commitment to corporate social responsibility and community development. As our esteemed partner, IOI Group shares our vision of fostering a healthy and vibrant community through sports. With their support, we are able to expand our reach and provide valuable opportunities for individuals to participate in badminton and other recreational activities. Together, we strive to create a more inclusive and active society.
